Virtual Meeting Instructor Guide

A facilitator's companion for leading missionaries through all 39 principles. Each entry includes suggested timing, a discussion question to open the group, a role-play scenario to run in pairs, and instructor notes on what to watch for.

How to Use This in a Virtual Meeting

  • Before the meeting: Select 2-4 principles to cover per session based on where your missionaries most need strengthening. Do not try to cover an entire lesson in one meeting.
  • Opening (5 min): Open with prayer, then briefly state which principles you'll cover and why you chose them today.
  • For each principle (8-12 min): Ask the discussion question to the group → hear 2-3 responses → briefly teach the key points → run the role-play in pairs (3-4 min) → debrief together (2 min).
  • Closing (5 min): Invite missionaries to share one thing they'll apply in their next teaching visit. Close with testimony and prayer.
  • Student Guide: Missionaries should have their Deep Dive file open during the meeting for reference. They are the student version; this is yours.
