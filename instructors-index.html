The Framework, in Brief

  1. Find the one sentence. Before teaching, know the single idea you want to land.
  2. Ask, then wait. One simple question, followed by real silence.
  3. Extend a natural next step. A commitment that grows directly from the principle, with a follow-up planned.
  4. Always return to the heart. Whatever the lesson, it's an invitation to come closer to Jesus Christ.
