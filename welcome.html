What This Training Is

This is a four-section foundational skills course, followed by an in-depth doctrinal companion for all four PMG lessons. It is designed to be used alongside your virtual training meetings, which are held two to three times a week. Each section focuses on one specific teaching skill that will directly improve your lessons with investigators.

What Is Expected of You

  • Attend every virtual training meeting — two to three times per week.
  • Study the assigned section or deep dive before each meeting.
  • Participate in role-plays during meetings — this is where the real learning happens.
  • Apply one specific thing from each session in your very next teaching visit.
  • Come to the next meeting ready to share what you tried and what happened.
A note on scripture study: This training uses scriptures from the Bible and Book of Mormon when teaching investigators. The Doctrine & Covenants is referenced for your own study and understanding as a missionary, but is not typically used directly with investigators. The Deep Dive sections will always indicate which scriptures are for which purpose.

One Last Thing

No training system makes a great missionary. The Spirit does. What this training does is clear away the habits and gaps that can get in the way of the Spirit working through you. Study it, apply it, and then trust God to do what only He can do. The people of this mission are waiting for you.
