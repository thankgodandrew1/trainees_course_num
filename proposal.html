Proposal for Mission President Authorization

Teaching with Clarity & Compassion — New Missionary Foundations Training
To: President Richard Paapa Dadzie, Nigeria Uyo Mission
From: Elder Andrew and Elder Edison-Mudor, Assistants to the President
Date: June 2026

The Need

New missionaries in this mission arrive with sincere testimonies and a genuine desire to teach. However, a consistent pattern of skill gaps has been observed across the mission: lessons that drift between principles, questions that confuse rather than open dialogue, and commitments that feel disconnected from what was taught. These gaps limit their effectiveness, particularly in the critical first weeks of their service.

"Seek not to declare my word, but first seek to obtain my word, and then shall your tongue be loosed; then, if you desire, you shall have my Spirit and my word, yea, the power of God unto the convincing of men." D&C 11:21

The Proposal

We are requesting authorization to implement a structured teaching foundations course for missionaries primarily in their second six weeks of training, delivered through virtual meetings held two to three times per week, facilitated by the Assistants to the President. All materials are developed, digitally accessible from the training homepage at Training Homepage, and ready for immediate deployment.

Course Structure

Component Focus When
Section 1: One Principle, Clearly Taught Focus; avoiding drift Week 1
Section 2: Questions That Open the Heart Simple questions; listening Week 1
Section 3: Commitments That Lead to Christ Natural invitations; follow-up Week 2
Section 4: The Heart of Each Lesson Core message distillation Week 2
Deep Dive: Lessons 1-3 Doctrinal depth; investigator questions Weeks 3-4
Deep Dive: Lesson 4 Lifelong discipleship principles Weeks 5-6
Quick Reference Companion All 39 principles; ongoing prep tool Ongoing

Key Points

  • Target audience: primarily missionaries in their second six weeks of training, with support available for first-six-week missionaries as needed.
  • All content is aligned with Preach My Gospel (2023 edition), Chapter 3.
  • Bible and Book of Mormon scriptures used for investigator teaching; no deviation from approved PMG lesson structure.
  • No financial cost. No additional personnel required.
  • Facilitated entirely by the Assistants to the President under the Mission President's direction.

Expected Outcome

Within six weeks, missionaries completing this course are expected to teach with greater clarity and focus, ask better questions, extend more meaningful commitments, and handle investigator questions with confidence — resulting in stronger investigator progression mission-wide.

We humbly submit this proposal for your consideration and prayerful review, President Dadzie. We are available to discuss it at your convenience and will proceed only upon your authorization and direction.
