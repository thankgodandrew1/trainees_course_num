Deep Dive: Lesson 4 — Becoming Lifelong Disciples

All 17 principles, in the same format as Lessons 1-3: what to help the person understand, Bible & Book of Mormon scriptures, common teaching breakdowns, real investigator questions with simple responses, how each connects to the rest of the gospel, and a testimony prompt.

A note on this lesson: Lesson 4 is taught over several visits, organized around the baptismal covenants (take upon us the name of Christ, keep the commandments, serve God and others, endure to the end). Many of these principles are sensitive (chastity, family situations, past mistakes) — the "Common Drift" sections especially emphasize sensitivity and pacing, not just doctrinal accuracy.
