Teaching with Clarity & Compassion

A foundations training module to help missionaries teach with clarity, ask questions that open hearts, extend meaningful commitments, and distill the core message of every lesson — so investigators can come closer to Christ.

How to use this module: Each section builds on the last, but you can also jump to whichever area your companionship needs most right now. Sections work for self-paced study or as the basis for a live virtual training — plan for about 15–20 minutes per section.

Recommended order: work through Sections 1–3 to build the core skills, then use Section 4 to bring everything together across the four major lesson areas.

The Framework, in Brief

  1. Find the one sentence. Before teaching, know the single idea you want to land.
  2. Ask, then wait. One simple question, followed by real silence.
  3. Extend a natural next step. A commitment that grows directly from the principle, with a follow-up planned.
  4. Always return to the heart. Whatever the lesson, it's an invitation to come closer to Jesus Christ.
